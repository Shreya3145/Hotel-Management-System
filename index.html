Immerse yourself in the grandeur of Glacier, the epitome of luxury resorts in Pondicherry, boasting one of the largest swimming pools on the eastern coast of India. Tucked away from the hustle and bustle of city life, this iconic Pondicherry resort offers a tranquil retreat within its expansive 23-acre grounds, featuring a man-made lake that cradles our inviting pool. Each room, adorned with elegant furnishings, ensures a captivating view of the mesmerizing lake, while every villa boasts a private balcony that gracefully extends over the lakefront. The lush gardens and verdant surroundings create a peaceful oasis, transforming each villa into a serene sanctuary. Enhancing the charm are the resort's flexible banquet facilities and lively discotheque, ensuring that every aspect of your stay is both convenient and enchanting. Welcome to Glacier, a resort boasting Pondicherry's largest island pool, where luxury seamlessly merges with nature to curate unforgettable experiences.
